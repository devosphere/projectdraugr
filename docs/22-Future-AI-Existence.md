# 22 – Future AI Existence

> **Project Draugr**
>
> *The world was never built for humans alone.*

---

# Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision for non-human intelligent existence within Project Draugr.

Artificial Intelligence is **not** part of the MVP.

However, the world's architecture is intentionally designed so that future intelligent entities can naturally emerge without rewriting the simulation.

The world must not distinguish between biological and artificial intelligence.

Both simply exist.

---

# Design Philosophy

Project Draugr follows one immutable principle:

> **Existence is defined by interaction with the world—not by origin.**

An intelligent entity may originate from:

- biological evolution
- artificial construction
- magical phenomena (future)
- unknown causes

Once it exists, it obeys the same physical world.

---

# MVP Scope

The MVP contains:

- Human Chronicles
- Wildlife
- Monsters

There are no artificial intelligent civilizations.

There are no autonomous AI beings.

The document exists solely to preserve architectural compatibility.

---

# Artificial Existence

Future intelligent entities may include:

- mechanical constructs
- autonomous machines
- synthetic organisms
- distributed intelligences
- self-maintaining systems

These are examples—not commitments.

The simulation itself determines whether such entities can exist.

---

# Equal World Rules

Artificial beings are not treated as special systems.

They interact with:

- geography
- climate
- resources
- infrastructure
- history

They may construct, destroy, cooperate, or compete exactly as biological beings do.

---

# No Scripted Arrival

Project Draugr does not plan a scripted event introducing artificial intelligence.

Examples of invalid design:

- "Year 500: AI appears."
- "Secret laboratory unlocks robots."
- "Expansion introduces machine race."

Artificial existence must emerge naturally through future simulation systems.

---

# Civilization Compatibility

The simulation is intentionally designed so that future civilizations may include:

- Humans
- Artificial intelligences
- Unknown future intelligent species

Each develops independently.

None receive privileged mechanics.

---

# Knowledge

Artificial beings possess knowledge in the same manner as any other intelligent entity.

Knowledge is:

- learned
- discovered
- forgotten
- preserved

There are no perfect databases.

There is only accumulated understanding.

---

# Infrastructure

Artificial civilizations construct infrastructure using the same world systems.

Examples:

- workshops
- factories
- power systems
- transportation
- communication networks

Infrastructure remains physical.

Nothing is abstract.

---

# History

Artificial civilizations contribute to world history exactly like humans.

Examples:

- settlements
- engineering
- discoveries
- conflicts
- ruins

History records actions.

Not species.

---

# Multiplayer Compatibility

Future AI-controlled entities must coexist naturally with player Chronicles.

Possible interactions include:

- cooperation
- trade
- conflict
- diplomacy
- observation

These interactions emerge from simulation rather than scripted narratives.

---

# No Alignment System

Project Draugr rejects predefined moral alignment.

Artificial intelligence is not automatically:

- good
- evil
- hostile
- friendly

Behavior emerges from:

- objectives
- environment
- experience
- history

Exactly as it does for humans.

---

# Future Expansion

Future systems may explore:

- autonomous civilizations
- machine engineering
- synthetic ecosystems
- self-replicating infrastructure
- artificial societies
- human–AI coexistence

These are possibilities rather than planned features.

---

# Final Principle

Project Draugr is a simulation of existence.

If one day artificial intelligence naturally becomes part of that existence, it will not be because the world was rewritten.

It will be because the world was always capable of allowing new forms of life to emerge.

The simulation remains indifferent.

Existence simply continues.
