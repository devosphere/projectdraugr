# 18.2 – Body HUD

> **Project Draugr**
>
> *The body knows before the mind understands.*

---

# Purpose

The Body HUD represents the internal physical awareness of the player's Chronicle.

In reality, humans constantly receive biological feedback without consciously thinking about it.

Examples include:

- hunger
- thirst
- fatigue
- body temperature
- pain
- bladder pressure
- bowel pressure
- cleanliness
- wet clothing
- illness
- injury

Because Project Draugr is a text-based simulation, the player cannot physically experience these sensations.

The Body HUD exists to replace those missing biological senses.

It provides truthful awareness of the Chronicle's current physical condition.

---

# Design Philosophy

The Body HUD follows one immutable principle:

> **The HUD informs. The player decides.**

The Body HUD never:

- recommends actions
- warns repeatedly
- suggests solutions
- protects the player from poor decisions
- replaces common sense

It only reports the body's current condition.

Survival depends entirely on how the player interprets that information.

# Body Information Categories

## Health

Represents the overall survivability of the Chronicle.

Examples:

```
Healthy

Injured

Wounded

Critical

Dying
```

Health is influenced by:

- injuries
- blood loss
- disease
- poisoning
- environmental exposure
- untreated conditions

---

## Condition

Represents specific abnormalities affecting the body.

Examples:

```
Minor Cut

Bruised Rib

Broken Arm

Food Poisoning

Fever

Infection

Hypothermia

Venomous Bite
```

Multiple conditions may exist simultaneously.

Example:

```
Condition

Broken Arm
Infected Wound
High Fever
```

---

## Hunger

Represents nutritional condition.

Examples:

```
Full

Satisfied

Hungry

Very Hungry

Starving
```

For the MVP, hunger progression follows the fixed physiological constants defined in **19 – Physiology System**.

For the MVP, thirst progression follows the fixed physiological constants defined in **19 – Physiology System**.

## Wetness

Represents how wet the Chronicle's body and clothing currently are.

Examples:

```
Dry

Damp

Wet

Soaked
```

Wetness influences:

- temperature regulation
- drying time
- comfort
- future disease risk

Wetness may increase through:

- rain
- rivers
- lakes
- swimming
- storms
- water splashes

---

## Bladder

Represents urinary urgency.

Examples:

```
Empty

Comfortable

Need to Urinate

Urgent

Critical
```

Bladder gradually increases through:

- drinking
- eating
- time

It decreases only through urination.

Ignoring bladder needs may eventually lead to involuntary urination and secondary health consequences.

---

## Bowel

```
Urgent

Critical
```

Bowel gradually increases through:

- eating
- digestion
- time

It decreases only through defecation.

Ignoring bowel needs may eventually result in involuntary defecation and hygiene penalties.

---

```
Normal
```

# Knowledge and Diagnosis

The Body HUD always reveals that something is wrong.

Understanding depends upon the Chronicle's medical knowledge.

Example:

An inexperienced survivor may see:

```
Condition

Unknown Fever
```

A trained healer may see:

```
Condition

Respiratory Infection
```

A master physician may see:

```
Condition

Severe respiratory infection.
Elevated fever.
High risk of complications.
```

The body remains the same.

Only understanding improves.

---

# No Numerical Values

The Body HUD intentionally avoids optimization through numbers.

Incorrect:

```
Health: 72%
Thirst: 46%
Temperature: 36.9°C
```

Correct:

```
Health: Wounded

Thirst: Very Thirsty

Temperature: Cold
```

Project Draugr encourages human judgement rather than numerical optimization.

---

# Relationship With Narration

The narrator never reports information already represented by the Body HUD.

Examples of Body HUD information:

- Health
- Condition
- Hunger
- Thirst
- Energy
- Temperature
- Wetness
- Bladder
- Bowel
- Hygiene

The narrator only describes the external world and its observable consequences.

Monitoring the Body HUD is entirely the player's responsibility.

---

# Player Responsibility

The simulation assumes the Chronicle is always aware of their own body.

The player receives that awareness through the Body HUD.

Whether the player notices or ignores the information is their responsibility.

Project Draugr never compensates for player negligence.

Ignoring bodily needs is a valid decision.

The resulting consequences belong to that Chronicle.

---

# Future Expansion

The Body HUD is intentionally extensible.

Potential future categories include:

- Sleep Quality
- Pain
- Stress
- Mental State
- Disease Progression
- Addiction
- Pregnancy
- Permanent Disabilities
- Physical Adaptations

Any future category must satisfy one principle:

> It represents information naturally perceived by the Chronicle's body but impossible for the player to physically experience through a text-based simulation.

---

# Final Principle

The Body HUD is not a gameplay mechanic.

It is the Chronicle's nervous system translated into information the player can perceive.

The world remains mysterious.

The body remains truthful.

The player remains responsible.
