# 18.8 – Chronicle UI

> **Project Draugr**
>
> *Every Chronicle is a life. Every life leaves a legacy.*

---

# Purpose

The Chronicle UI presents the identity and historical record of the current Chronicle.

Unlike traditional RPG character sheets, the Chronicle UI is not designed to display statistics.

Instead, it documents the life that the Chronicle has lived.

It answers questions such as:

- Who am I?
- When was I born into this world?
- How long have I survived?
- What have I accomplished?
- What legacy will I leave behind?

---

# Design Philosophy

Project Draugr follows one immutable principle:

> **A Chronicle is a life—not a character slot.**

The Chronicle UI records history.

It does not exist to optimize builds or compare numerical attributes.

A Chronicle's value comes from its experiences.

---

# Opening the Chronicle UI

The Chronicle UI appears only when intentionally requested.

Examples:

```text id="nct0fd"
Open Chronicle.

Show Chronicle.

View Chronicle.
```

The UI never opens automatically.

# Chronicle Identity

Each Chronicle possesses a unique identity.

Core information includes:

- Name
- Chronicle ID
- Creation Date
- Survival Time
- Current Status

The Chronicle ID uniquely identifies that life throughout the world's history.

---

# Status

Possible states include:

```text id="lh3gb0"
Alive

Missing

Dead
```

Dead Chronicles remain permanently recorded.

They are never deleted.

---

# Survival Time

The UI displays how long the Chronicle has survived.

Example:

```text id="30xgzy"
Current Age

14 Days
```

Time continues advancing naturally until death.

---

# Current Location

Displays the Chronicle's most recent known location.

Example:

```text id="jsshoj"
Current Location

Northern Pine Forest
```

Future systems may support more precise geographic references.

---

# Achievements

Achievements are not game rewards.

They are historical milestones.

Examples:

```text id="n38dmc"
Achievements

• Built First Shelter

• Crafted First Rope

• Crossed the Great River

• Established First Workshop
```

Achievements describe events rather than progression.

---

# Discoveries

Records significant first-time discoveries.

Examples:

```text id="e0nhpm"
Discoveries

• Red Cap Mushroom

• Limestone Cave

• Wild Boar Herd

• Ancient Stone Ruins
```

Discoveries become part of the Chronicle's personal history.

---

# Statistics

Statistics summarize the Chronicle's lifetime.

Examples:

```text id="l6x8dw"
Statistics

Days Survived

Distance Walked

Structures Built

Items Crafted

Animals Hunted

Trees Felled

Campfires Lit
```

Statistics exist for historical interest rather than competition.

---

# Personal Notes

Chronicles may maintain personal notes.

Example:

```text id="ydr3mg"
Personal Notes

Avoid crossing the river during heavy rain.

Wild boars frequently appear near the eastern meadow.
```

These notes are private records belonging to the Chronicle.

Future systems may allow journals to become physical objects.

---

# Death

Upon death:

The Chronicle immediately transitions to:

```text id="kju0vi"
Status

Dead
```

The record becomes read-only.

No further updates occur.

The Chronicle can never be resumed.

---

# Legacy

Every Chronicle permanently contributes to the world's history.

Its legacy may include:

- structures built
- crafted objects
- journals
- discoveries
- maps
- infrastructure
- settlements

Future Chronicles may encounter these remnants.

The Chronicle itself never returns.

---

# Future Expansion

The Chronicle UI may later include:

- family lineage
- titles earned
- reputation
- notable events
- timeline visualization
- cause of death
- memorial page
- historical relationships

These additions must preserve the same philosophy.

---

# Final Principle

The Chronicle UI is not a character sheet.

It is the biography of a human life lived within Project Draugr.

Every Chronicle begins as an unknown survivor.

Only its choices determine the story it leaves behind.
