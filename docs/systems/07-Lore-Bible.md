# 07 – Lore Bible

> **Project Draugr**
>
> *The truths behind the world.*

---

# Purpose

This document defines the canonical lore of Project Draugr.

Unlike gameplay documentation, the Lore Bible contains knowledge available only to the project's creators.

Most of the information contained within this document is never directly presented to players.

Players discover only fragments of truth through exploration, history, and observation.

The complete truth belongs only to the world itself.

---

# The Nature of the World

Project Draugr takes place within a real, independent universe existing parallel to Earth.

It is not:

- a simulation
- a virtual world
- a dream
- a purgatory
- an afterlife
- a parallel Earth

It is an entirely separate reality.

It existed before humanity arrived.

It will continue existing after humanity disappears.

---

# Humanity

Human beings are not native to this world.

Every player represents an ordinary human transported from Earth.

No one is chosen.

No one possesses destiny.

No one is summoned because of special ability.

Transported humans simply awaken in an unfamiliar world.

Why this happens remains one of the greatest mysteries.

---

# The World Before Humanity

Before the arrival of transported humans, the world already contained:

- continents
- oceans
- forests
- rivers
- mountains
- wildlife
- monsters
- forgotten civilizations
- ruins
- ancient relics

The world has experienced countless ages before the first Chronicle begins.

Humanity arrives late in its history.

---

# Ancient Civilization

Long before transported humans appeared, intelligent civilizations flourished.

They built:

- cities
- temples
- roads
- monuments
- underground structures
- unknown technologies

Today, only ruins remain.

The exact cause of their disappearance is intentionally undefined.

Whether they perished...

...ascended...

...or transformed...

remains unknown.

Their history survives only through what they left behind.

---

# The Two Primordial Principles

Before recorded history, two eternal principles already existed.

## Creation

Creation represents emergence.

Growth.

Birth.

Formation.

Potential.

Renewal.

Creation allows existence to become.

---

## Destruction

Destruction represents transformation.

Decay.

Death.

Collapse.

Consumption.

Change.

Destruction allows existence to continue evolving.

---

# Equilibrium

Neither Creation nor Destruction is superior.

Neither seeks domination.

Existence itself emerges only because both remain in balance.

If Creation existed alone...

the world would endlessly accumulate.

Nothing would ever die.

Nothing would ever change.

If Destruction existed alone...

nothing could endure.

Nothing could grow.

Nothing could exist.

The world continues because both principles coexist.

---

# The World Does Not Obey Them

Creation and Destruction do not actively rule the world.

They do not command reality.

Instead, the world's natural systems continuously express their balance.

Growth.

Decay.

Birth.

Death.

Construction.

Collapse.

These are not acts of divine intervention.

They are the natural language of existence.

---

# The Overseer

Beyond the world exists one eternal observer.

The Overseer.

The Overseer is not a god.

Not a ruler.

Not a creator.

Not a destroyer.

It exists outside the world's hierarchy.

Its purpose is singular.

To observe.

To remember.

Every Chronicle.

Every civilization.

Every discovery.

Every failure.

Every extinction.

Every beginning.

Nothing escapes its memory.

---

# The Chronicles

A Chronicle is more than a save file.

A Chronicle is one complete human existence.

It begins when a transported human awakens.

It concludes with death.

Everything accomplished during that life becomes part of the world's permanent history whenever technically possible.

Every Chronicle changes the world.

No Chronicle changes history itself.

---

# History

History is the greatest treasure of Project Draugr.

Buildings decay.

Roads weather.

Kingdoms collapse.

Forests reclaim forgotten cities.

Yet history remains.

Future generations inherit the consequences of previous lives.

Nothing meaningful is ever completely lost.

---

# Knowledge

Knowledge is humanity's greatest advantage.

Transported humans arrive with memories from Earth.

Those memories become their greatest initial tool.

Fire.

Agriculture.

Engineering.

Medicine.

Navigation.

Construction.

The world does not grant power.

Knowledge creates it.

---

# Civilization

Civilization is not predetermined.

It is neither guaranteed nor protected.

Human civilization exists only because transported humans choose to create it.

Every settlement is an act of hope.

Every ruined city is a reminder that hope eventually fades.

Civilization itself is transient.

History is permanent.

---

# Monsters

Monsters are natural inhabitants of the world.

They are not embodiments of evil.

They are not supernatural punishments.

They occupy ecological roles just as wildlife does.

Some are predators.

Some are scavengers.

Some are territorial.

Some are passive.

Human morality has no influence over their existence.

---

# Death

Death is never treated as failure.

Death is the natural conclusion of existence.

Every Chronicle eventually ends.

The value of a Chronicle is determined not by its length...

...but by the mark it leaves upon the world.

---

# Mystery

Not every mystery should be solved.

Some questions intentionally remain unanswered.

Examples include:

- Why are humans transported?
- Who built the ancient civilization?
- Why did they disappear?
- What lies beyond the known continents?
- Why does the Overseer remember?
- Why do Creation and Destruction exist?

The unknown gives the world depth.

Mystery is preserved deliberately.

---

# Canon Rules

The following truths are immutable.

- The world predates humanity.
- Humanity is not native.
- Civilization is entirely player-created.
- Creation and Destruction remain in equilibrium.
- The Overseer never intervenes.
- History is permanent.
- Death concludes every Chronicle.
- The world continues regardless of human existence.

Future lore expansions must remain consistent with these truths.

---

# Final Principle

Project Draugr is not built upon prophecy.

Nor destiny.

Nor heroes.

It is built upon one simple truth:

The world existed before humanity.

The world will remain after humanity.

For a brief moment, each Chronicle becomes part of that endless history.

Every life leaves a mark.

The world remembers.
