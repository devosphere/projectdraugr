# Purpose

This document defines the Ecosystem System within Project Draugr.

It establishes how living organisms exist, interact, adapt, and change within the world.

The purpose of this system is to create a world where life behaves according to natural rules rather than existing only as gameplay resources.

---

# Design Philosophy

Life survives.

The world of Project Draugr has suffered a catastrophic transformation, but destruction does not mean the absence of life.

Life adapts.

Life changes.

Life finds opportunities.

---

# Core Principle

## The World Is Not Dead. It Is Different.

The apocalypse did not erase life.

It altered:

- environments;
- species;
- relationships;
- survival patterns.

---

# Ecosystem Structure

The ecosystem consists of:

```
Environment

↓

Resources

↓

Plants

↓

Animals

↓

Predators

↓

Intelligent Life

↓

Civilization
```

Every layer influences the others.

# Ecological Relationships

The world follows interconnected relationships.

Examples:

Plants provide:

- food;
- shelter;
- resources.

Animals provide:

- ecological balance;
- materials;
- environmental influence.

Predators control:

- population;
- migration;
- ecosystem stability.

---

# Plant Life

Plants exist according to environmental conditions.

They may provide:

- food;
- medicine;
- materials;
- resources.

---

# Plant Adaptation

Plants may adapt to:

- climate;
- corruption;
- soil conditions;
- environmental pressure.

Examples:

A corrupted forest may produce:

- dangerous plants;
- unusual materials;
- altered biology.

---

# Animal Life

Animals exist as living entities.

They have:

- habitats;
- behaviors;
- survival needs;
- population changes.

Animals are not simply enemies or resources.

---

# Animal Behavior

Creatures may:

- hunt;
- migrate;
- reproduce;
- avoid danger;
- compete for resources.

Behavior depends on:

- species;
- environment;
- current conditions.

---

# Predator and Prey System

The ecosystem maintains natural balance.

Examples:

If predators disappear:

- prey populations increase;
- vegetation decreases;
- ecosystem changes.

If prey decreases:

- predators migrate;
- populations decline.

---

# Marine Ecosystem

The oceans represent a major part of the world.

Marine life may include:

- surviving species;
- mutated organisms;
- unknown creatures;
- deep-sea ecosystems.

The ocean should not be treated as empty space.

---

# Corruption Influence

Corruption affects ecosystems.

Possible effects:

- mutations;
- environmental changes;
- altered behavior;
- new survival patterns.

Corruption does not simply destroy life.

It creates different forms of existence.

---

# Adaptation System

Life may adapt over generations.

Examples:

Creatures may develop:

- resistance;
- new behaviors;
- physical changes.

Plants may develop:

- survival mechanisms;
- altered growth patterns.

---

# Extinction

Species may disappear.

Causes include:

- environmental collapse;
- overhunting;
- corruption;
- climate changes.

Extinction should be possible.

The world remembers what is lost.

---

# Resource Relationship

Resources are part of the ecosystem.

Examples:

Overharvesting an area may cause:

- reduced regeneration;
- animal migration;
- ecosystem imbalance.

---

# Player Impact

The Awakened Soul can influence ecosystems.

Examples:

Positive impact:

- restoring forests;
- protecting wildlife;
- managing resources.

Negative impact:

- overhunting;
- pollution;
- destruction.

---

# Farming and Domestication

The Awakened Soul may eventually influence ecosystems through:

- agriculture;
- animal domestication;
- cultivation.

These require knowledge and skill.

They are not automatically available.

---

# Knowledge and Observation

Understanding ecosystems requires learning.

The Awakened Soul may discover:

- animal patterns;
- plant uses;
- environmental relationships.

Knowledge creates opportunity.

# Ecosystem and Survival

Survival depends on understanding nature.

Examples:

A knowledgeable survivor understands:

- safe food sources;
- animal behavior;
- weather patterns;
- resource cycles.

An ignorant survivor takes risks.

# Developer Notes

The ecosystem should avoid being a simple database of creatures.

Every living thing should have:

- a reason to exist;
- a place in the world;
- a relationship with other life.

---

# Edge Cases

## Rare Species

Rare life forms should exist naturally.

They should not exist only as collectibles.

---

## Player Extinction Events

Players should be able to negatively affect local ecosystems.

However, large-scale extinction should require significant impact.

---

## Unknown Creatures

Unknown life should follow biological logic.

Mystery should not break consistency.

The world of Project Draugr is not a collection of resources waiting to be harvested.

The Awakened Soul is only one life among countless others struggling to exist.
