# Final Statement

The future is not simply where civilization goes.

It is what civilization becomes.

The greatest achievement is not reaching another planet.

It is reaching a new understanding of existence.

The descendants of civilization may not look like their ancestors.

But every future form carries the memory of those who came before.

The question of the future is not:

"Will we survive?"

It is:

"What will we choose to become?"
