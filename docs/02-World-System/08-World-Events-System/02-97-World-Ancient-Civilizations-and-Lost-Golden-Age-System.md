# Purpose

This document defines ancient civilizations and lost golden ages.

It establishes:

- ancient societies;
- forgotten empires;
- golden age periods;
- lost technologies;
- historical legacy.

---

# Design Philosophy

Civilizations do not disappear completely.

Their knowledge, mistakes, and dreams continue through those who follow.

---

# Core Principle

## Every Civilization Leaves A Shadow Behind, Even After Its Light Has Faded.

---

# Ancient Civilization Definition

Ancient civilizations are societies that emerged after the First Era and developed advanced cultural, technological, or existential achievements.
