# Purpose

This document defines the existence, behavior, and influence of primordial beings and ancient entities.

It establishes:

- primordial life forms;
- ancient civilizations;
- creator-class entities;
- forgotten powers;
- legendary beings.

---

# Design Philosophy

The oldest beings should represent mystery, not simply power.

---

# Core Principle

## The First Beings Are Not The Strongest Because They Rule. They Are Powerful Because They Remember.

---

# Primordial Being Definition

A primordial being is an entity that existed near the earliest stages of reality.

---

# Characteristics

Possible traits:

- extreme age;
- ancient knowledge;
- unique existence;
- connection to reality foundations.

---

# Ancient Entity Definition

An ancient entity is a being from an earlier age whose existence influenced history.
