# Purpose

This document defines how conflicts develop between individuals, groups, and civilizations.

It establishes:

- conflict causes;
- warfare;
- military systems;
- strategies;
- consequences;
- post-war evolution.

---

# Design Philosophy

Conflict is a force of change.

---

# Core Principle

## Wars Do Not Only Destroy Worlds. They Reshape Them.

---

# Conflict Definition

Conflict occurs when opposing interests cannot be resolved peacefully.

---

# Conflict Levels

```
Personal Conflict

↓

Community Conflict

↓

Regional Conflict

↓

Civilization Conflict

↓

World Conflict
```

---

# Personal Conflict

Conflict between individuals.

Examples:

- rivalry;
- disputes;
- betrayal.

---

# Community Conflict

Conflict between groups.

Examples:

- territory disputes;
- resource competition.

---

# Regional Conflict

Conflict involving multiple settlements.

Examples:

- border conflicts;
- political struggles.

---

# Civilization Conflict

Large-scale conflicts.

Examples:

- wars between nations;
- ideological conflicts.

---

# World Conflict

Events affecting many civilizations.

Examples:

- global wars;
- existential threats.

# Final Statement

War is not only about who wins.

It is about what remains afterward.

Every battlefield creates memories.

Every victory creates expectations.

Every loss creates stories.

The Awakened Soul exists in a world where civilizations struggle, adapt, and change.

Conflict is not the end of history.

Conflict is one of the forces that creates it.
