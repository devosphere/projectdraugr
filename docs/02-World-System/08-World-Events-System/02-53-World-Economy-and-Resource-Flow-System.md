# Purpose

This document defines how resources, goods, and economic activity flow throughout the world.

It establishes:

- resource generation;
- production;
- consumption;
- trade;
- scarcity;
- economic change.

---

# Design Philosophy

Economies are living systems.

---

# Core Principle

## Resources Move Through The World According To Need, Availability, And Opportunity.

---

# Economy Definition

The economy represents the exchange and management of:

- resources;
- goods;
- services;
- labor;
- knowledge.

```

+

Agricultural Resources

+

Manufactured Goods

+

Knowledge Resources

+

Rare Resources
```

# Agricultural Resources

Examples:

- crops;

- food products.

# Manufactured Goods

- unique minerals;
- ancient artifacts;
- unusual substances.

```
↓

↓

Consumption
```

# Final Statement

Civilizations are built from resources.

Cities rise because materials arrive.

People survive because food flows.

Knowledge spreads because trade connects distant places.

The Awakened Soul enters a world where value is constantly moving.

They may redirect the flow.

They may benefit from it.

They may disrupt it.

But the economy of the world continues with or without them.
