# Purpose

This document defines Awakening, the moment when an Incoming Soul regains consciousness within the new world and officially begins their Chronicle.

Awakening is the first true experience of existence after transportation.

It is the moment an Incoming Soul becomes an Awakened Soul.

---

# Design Philosophy

Awakening is not a tutorial.

It is not a scripted introduction.

It is not a protected starting area.

It is the first moment of survival.

The world does not explain itself.

The Awakened Soul must observe, learn, and adapt.

---

# Overview

After transportation concludes, consciousness returns.

The Awakened Soul opens their eyes somewhere within the world.

They do not know:

- where they are;
- why they are there;
- how they arrived;
- what happened to Earth;
- what dangers surround them.

They possess only:

- their memories;
- their knowledge;
- their clothing;
- their ability to think.

The Chronicle begins.

---

# The First Moment

The first experience after awakening should focus on immersion.

The Awakened Soul may perceive:

- unfamiliar surroundings;
- unknown weather;
- unfamiliar sounds;
- strange landscapes;
- environmental dangers.

The world should feel real before it feels understandable.

---

# Awakening Location

Every Awakened Soul begins within an existing location.

The Awakening Location is determined by:

- world state;
- environmental conditions;
- time;
- geography;
- narrative context.

The location is never created specifically for the Awakened Soul.

---

# Initial Awareness

Upon awakening, the Awakened Soul begins with limited understanding.

They know:

- they are alive;
- they are no longer on Earth;
- something impossible occurred.

They do not automatically know:

- the world's history;
- the world's dangers;
- available resources;
- local civilizations;
- survival methods.

Knowledge must be acquired.

---

# No Forced Introduction

The world does not provide:

- a narrator explaining events;
- a quest marker;
- a mission objective;
- a guided tutorial;
- a guaranteed companion.

The Awakened Soul determines their own first action.

Possible first actions:

- observe surroundings;
- search for resources;
- seek shelter;
- investigate nearby areas;
- attempt communication;
- begin traveling.

Every choice begins shaping the Chronicle.

---

# First Survival Considerations

Immediately after awakening, the Awakened Soul must consider:

## Environment

What surrounds them?

Is the area:

- safe;
- hostile;
- corrupted;
- resource-rich;
- barren?

---

## Physical Condition

The Awakened Soul must understand:

- hunger;
- thirst;
- fatigue;
- injuries;
- exposure.

---

## Resources

The Awakened Soul must identify:

- water sources;
- shelter opportunities;
- materials;
- possible food.

---

# Interface Philosophy

The Awakened Soul should not immediately understand all available systems.

Information should be discovered gradually.

The world should prioritize immersion over explanation.

Systems may reveal themselves through:

- observation;
- experimentation;
- learning;
- survival experience.

---

# The First Decision

The first decision of a Chronicle has no correct answer.

A cautious Awakened Soul may seek safety.

A curious Awakened Soul may explore.

A desperate Awakened Soul may take risks.

The world responds naturally.

---

# Integration With Other Systems

## Real-Time System

Awakening occurs according to the world's current date and time.

---

## World Continuity System

The Awakened Soul enters a world that has continued existing before their arrival.

---

## Survival Needs

The Awakened Soul must immediately manage basic survival requirements.

---

## Knowledge System

Understanding the world begins through observation and experience.

---

# Developer Notes

The opening moments should avoid overwhelming the player with information.

The goal is not to explain mechanics.

The goal is to create the feeling of being somewhere unknown.

Developers should prioritize:

- atmosphere;
- uncertainty;
- player curiosity;
- meaningful first decisions.

---

# Edge Cases

Awakening must:

- always occur after transportation;
- always provide player control;
- never place the Awakened Soul in an impossible situation;
- never guarantee safety.

The world can be dangerous.

But survival must always be logically possible.

---

# Future Expansion

Possible future additions:

- unique awakening scenarios;
- environmental storytelling;
- rare awakening locations;
- hidden connections between Chronicles.

These should enhance immersion without reducing uncertainty.

---

# Final Statement

The Arrival Protocol brought the Incoming Soul to the threshold.

Transportation crossed the boundary.

Awakening begins the Chronicle.

The world opens its eyes with them.
