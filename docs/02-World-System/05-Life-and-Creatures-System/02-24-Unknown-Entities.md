# Purpose

This document defines Unknown Entities within Project Draugr.

It establishes the classification and behavior of beings or phenomena that cannot be fully understood by normal life forms.

The purpose of this system is to preserve mystery while maintaining internal world consistency.

---

# Design Philosophy

Not everything in existence must be immediately understood.

A universe feels larger when knowledge has limits.

The Awakened Soul should experience moments where the correct response is not:

"What is this?"

but:

"What does this mean?"

---

# Core Principle

## Unknown Does Not Mean Unexplained.

Every entity has a reason to exist.

The limitation is not reality.

The limitation is understanding.

---

# Definition Of Unknown Entity

An Unknown Entity is an existence that cannot be fully classified using current knowledge.

It may appear as:

- a being;
- a creature;
- an object;
- a location;
- a natural event;
- a phenomenon.

---

# Classification

Unknown Entities are categorized by appearance.

---

# Entity Manifestation Types

## Physical Entity

A visible existence with physical interaction.

Examples:

- unusual creatures;
- ancient beings;
- unknown organisms.

---

## Environmental Entity

An entity expressed through the environment.

Examples:

- forests;
- mountains;
- rivers;
- locations.

---

## Phenomenon Entity

An entity expressed through events.

Examples:

- strange weather;
- impossible occurrences;
- unexplained signals.

---

## Conceptual Entity

An existence connected to ideas or principles.

Examples:

- memory;
- dreams;
- consciousness.

---

# Unknown Entity Behavior

Unknown Entities should still follow:

- consistent patterns;
- limitations;
- purposes.

---

# Encounter Philosophy

Unknown encounters should create:

- curiosity;
- uncertainty;
- reflection.

They should not exist only to create combat.

---

# Overseer Manifestations

The Overseer may interact with the Awakened Soul through unknown entities.

The Overseer is not limited to:

- humanoid appearance;
- voices;
- direct communication.

Possible manifestations:

- a tree;
- a creature;
- a location;
- a dream;
- a natural event;
- another intelligent being.

---

# Purpose Of Overseer Encounters

The Overseer may:

- observe;
- evaluate;
- test;
- guide;
- challenge.

The Awakened Soul should not immediately know the entity is the Overseer.

---

# Dream Encounters

Dreams may become a method of interaction.

Dream encounters may contain:

- questions;
- memories;
- symbolic events;
- evaluations.

Dreams should not reveal answers directly.

---

# Entity Evaluation

Unknown entities may evaluate the Awakened Soul through:

- choices;
- behavior;
- morality;
- survival decisions.

The evaluation is based on actions.

Not words.

---

# Entity Knowledge Levels

Understanding depends on the observer.

---

## Common Knowledge

Most beings may consider the entity:

- myth;
- unexplained event;
- legend.

---

## Research Knowledge

Scholars may discover:

- patterns;
- repeated events;
- connections.

---

## True Knowledge

Only beings with sufficient understanding may know:

- the entity's origin;
- its purpose;
- its relationship to the universe.

---

# Interaction Rules

Unknown Entities should not violate the world.

They must follow:

- universe laws;
- continuity;
- consequences.

---

# Player Interaction

The Awakened Soul may:

- ignore;
- investigate;
- cooperate;
- resist;
- misunderstand.

Every choice may affect future encounters.

---

# Entity Memory

Unknown Entities may remember.

They may remember:

- previous interactions;
- decisions;
- character history.

---

# Relationship With Chronicles

Unknown Entities may become part of a Chronicle.

A player may record:

- encounters;
- theories;
- discoveries.

Future Chronicles may find these records.

---

# Unknown Entities And Civilization

Civilizations may develop beliefs around unknown entities.

They may become:

- myths;
- gods;
- monsters;
- warnings.

---

# Unknown Entities And Corruption

Some unknown entities may be connected to corruption.

However:

Connection does not mean origin.

An entity may:

- study corruption;
- oppose corruption;
- use corruption;
- simply exist alongside it.

# Developer Notes

Unknown entities should be rare and meaningful.

Avoid making mystery meaningless through excessive randomness.

The player should remember encounters because they changed their understanding of the world.

## Entity Appears Hostile

---

## Entity Is A Location

A place itself may have purpose.
