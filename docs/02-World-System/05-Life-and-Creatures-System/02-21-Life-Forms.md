# Purpose

This document defines the Life Forms System within Project Draugr.

It establishes the classification, behavior, and existence of living beings within the universe.

The purpose of this system is to create a world where all life follows logical rules and contributes to the living ecosystem.

---

# Design Philosophy

Life exists independently from the player.

Creatures are not resources.

NPCs are not quest objects.

Entities are not obstacles.

Every living being has:

- an origin;
- a purpose;
- behaviors;
- survival needs.

---

# Core Principle

## Every Life Form Has A Reason To Exist.

Nothing exists only because the player needs it.

Life existed before the Awakened Soul arrived.

---

# Definition of Life Form

A life form is any biological or potentially biological entity capable of:

- existence;
- interaction;
- survival;
- adaptation.

---

Life forms are categorized into:

```

↓
```

- species;
- environment;

- adaptation.
