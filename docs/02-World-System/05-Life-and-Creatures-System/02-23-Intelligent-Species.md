# Purpose

This document defines intelligent species within Project Draugr.

It establishes the existence, characteristics, and behavior of intelligent beings capable of:

- reasoning;
- communication;
- culture;
- civilization building.

The purpose of this system is to create a universe containing diverse forms of intelligence.

---

# Design Philosophy

Intelligence is not exclusive to humanity.

Different species may experience the world differently.

They may have different:

- perceptions;
- values;
- societies;
- histories;
- beliefs.

---

# Core Principle

## Intelligence Creates Perspective.

Two intelligent species may witness the same event and understand it differently.

Neither perspective is automatically correct.

---

# Definition of Intelligent Species

An intelligent species is a life form capable of:

- abstract thought;
- communication;
- social organization;
- knowledge preservation;
- intentional decision-making.

---

# Intelligent Species Characteristics

Each intelligent species should define:

- biological traits;
- environmental adaptation;
- social structure;
- culture;
- history;
- relationship with other species.

---

# Species Origin

The origin of intelligent species may vary.

Possible origins:

- natural evolution;
- ancient adaptation;
- unknown cosmic events;
- consequences of corruption;
- unexplained phenomena.

The true origin may not be known by the species itself.

---

# Biological Diversity

Species may differ in:

- physical structure;
- lifespan;
- senses;
- reproduction;
- environmental needs.

---

# Perception Differences

Different species experience reality differently.

Examples:

One species may perceive:

- temperature changes;
- electromagnetic patterns;
- corruption influence;
- environmental signals.

Another species may not.

---

# Species Intelligence Types

Intelligence may appear in different forms.

---

# Individual Intelligence

Focused on personal survival and decision-making.

---

# Social Intelligence

Focused on:

- cooperation;
- communication;
- group behavior.

---

# Cultural Intelligence

Focused on:

- traditions;
- knowledge;
- civilization.

---

# Ancient Intelligence

Rare beings with:

- extreme lifespan;
- accumulated knowledge;
- unique understanding.

---

# Species Civilization

Intelligent species may form civilizations.

Civilizations may include:

- settlements;
- governments;
- traditions;
- economies;
- conflicts.

---

# Species Culture

Every intelligent species develops unique culture.

Culture may influence:

- morality;
- communication;
- technology;
- relationships.

---

# Species Relationship With Humans

Humans may not always be considered special.

Possible perspectives:

## Curiosity

Humans are viewed as unusual newcomers.

---

## Suspicion

Humans are seen as potential threats.

---

## Cooperation

Humans are accepted as another intelligent species.

---

## Fear

Humans are considered dangerous because of their unknown origin.

---

# Species Relationship With The Awakened Soul

The Awakened Soul may be interpreted differently.

Possible beliefs:

- lost traveler;
- anomaly;
- messenger;
- threat;
- opportunity.

---

# Species Knowledge Of Earth

Intelligent species generally have no knowledge of Earth.

The Awakened Soul's origin is a mystery.

Any recognition of Earth should be extremely rare and meaningful.

---

# Species And Corruption

Different species may have different relationships with corruption.

Examples:

## Resistant Species

May naturally tolerate corruption.

---

## Vulnerable Species

May suffer severe effects.

---

## Studying Species

May attempt to understand or control corruption.

---

## Worshiping Species

May interpret corruption spiritually.

---

# Species Conflict

Conflicts may occur because of:

- resources;
- territory;
- cultural differences;
- historical events.

---

# Species Cooperation

Cooperation may happen through:

- trade;
- alliances;
- shared threats;
- knowledge exchange.

---

# Language System

Intelligent species may have:

- unique languages;
- communication methods;
- writing systems.

Understanding language requires:

- learning;
- interaction;
- experience.

---

# Knowledge Preservation

Species preserve knowledge through:

- books;
- oral traditions;
- structures;
- artifacts;
- memories.

---

# Species Extinction

Intelligent species may disappear.

Causes:

- corruption;
- conflict;
- environmental collapse;
- unknown events.

Their existence should leave traces.

---

# Previous Chronicles And Intelligent Species

Future generations of intelligent species may remember previous Awakened Souls.

A player may become:

- a legend;
- a myth;
- a historical figure;
- a forgotten name.

---

# Overseer Relationship

Intelligent species may encounter the Overseer.

However, their interpretation depends on:

- culture;
- history;
- understanding.

One species may see guidance.

Another may see judgment.

# Developer Notes

Avoid creating intelligent species only as:

- enemies;
- allies;
- quest providers.

They should have their own goals.

They should continue existing without the player.

---

# Edge Cases

## Species Without Civilization

Intelligence does not require cities.

---

## Hidden Species

Some intelligent species may intentionally avoid civilization contact.

---

## Ancient Species

Ancient beings should not automatically know everything.

Knowledge has limits.

---

# Future Expansion

Potential future systems:

- species diplomacy;
- species evolution;
- language learning;
- multi-species civilizations;
- intelligent companion systems.

---

# Final Statement

The universe is larger than humanity.

The Awakened Soul did not enter a world waiting for them.

They entered a world where countless forms of life already had stories.

Some will welcome them.

Some will fear them.

Some will never understand them.

But all of them are part of the same Chronicle.
