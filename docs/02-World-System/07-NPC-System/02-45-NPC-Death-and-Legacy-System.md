# Purpose

This document defines how NPC death affects individuals, communities, civilizations, and world history.

It establishes:

- mortality;
- death consequences;
- inheritance;
- remembrance;
- legacy preservation.

---

# Design Philosophy

Death gives meaning to life.

---

# Core Principle

## Every Life Ends, But Its Impact Can Continue.

---

# NPC Mortality

NPCs are temporary inhabitants of the world.

They may experience:

- birth;
- growth;
- aging;
- decline;
- death.

---

# Death Is Natural

Death is not treated only as failure.

It is part of:

- ecology;
- society;
- history.

---

# Causes Of Death

NPCs may die from:

```
Natural Causes

+

Illness

+

Accidents

+

Environmental Dangers

+

Conflict

+

Player Actions
```

---

# Natural Death

Occurs through aging.

Factors:

- species;
- health;
- environment;
- lifestyle.

# Final Statement

Every person who exists in this world leaves something behind.

A name.

A memory.

A lesson.

A consequence.

The world does not remember everyone equally.

But every life contributes to the story of existence.

The Awakened Soul is not unique because they cannot die.

They are unique because they can choose what remains after they are gone.
