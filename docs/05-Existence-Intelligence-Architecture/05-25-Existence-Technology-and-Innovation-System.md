# Purpose

The Existence Technology and Innovation System defines how intelligent entities transform knowledge, creativity, and understanding into tools capable of changing their environment and expanding their capabilities.

This system governs:

- invention;
- scientific discovery;
- technological evolution;
- engineering;
- artificial intelligence;
- biological advancement;
- dimensional technologies;
- civilization technological progression;
- technology risks and responsibilities.
