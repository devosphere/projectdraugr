# Purpose

The Existence Universal Laws and Constants System defines the fundamental principles that govern all realities, dimensions, entities, and processes within the simulation.

This system establishes:

- universal laws;
- reality constants;
- existence principles;
- immutable rules;
- physical and metaphysical constraints;
- limitations of all entities;
- relationship between laws and freedom;
- relationship between laws and the Overseer.

---

# Core Principle

> **A universe without laws cannot sustain existence. Laws do not restrict existence; they provide the foundation that allows existence to emerge, evolve, and have meaning.**

---

# Universal Law Definition

A universal law is a fundamental rule that governs how existence behaves.

Universal laws determine:

- what can happen;
- what cannot happen;
- how entities interact;
- how reality maintains stability.

---

# Universal Constant Definition

A universal constant is a fixed property that remains consistent within a reality framework.

Examples:

- fundamental measurements;
- energy relationships;
- dimensional properties;
- existence parameters.
