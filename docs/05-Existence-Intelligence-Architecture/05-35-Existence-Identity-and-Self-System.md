# Purpose

The Existence Identity and Self System defines how entities develop, maintain, transform, and understand their sense of identity throughout existence.

This system governs:

- individual identity;
- self-awareness;
- continuity of existence;
- personality;
- species identity;
- cultural identity;
- civilization identity;
- artificial identity;
- collective identity;
- identity evolution.

---

# Core Principle

> **Identity is the pattern that allows an existence to recognize itself across change.**

---

# Identity Definition

Identity is the collection of characteristics, experiences, memories, relationships, values, and patterns that define an entity.

Identity answers:

- "What am I?"
- "What makes me different?"
- "What connects my past, present, and future?"
