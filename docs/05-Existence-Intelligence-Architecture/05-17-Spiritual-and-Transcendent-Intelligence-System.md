# Purpose

The Spiritual and Transcendent Intelligence System defines the highest layers of intelligence that exist beyond ordinary physical, biological, and computational limitations.

This system governs:

- spiritual existence;
- transcendence;
- ascension;
- higher consciousness;
- conceptual existence;
- primordial awareness;
- dimensional intelligence;
- entities beyond conventional physical existence.

---

# Core Principle

> **Transcendence is not the accumulation of power. It is the expansion of understanding, awareness, and relationship with existence itself.**

A transcendent entity is not defined by dominance.

It is defined by:

- perception beyond limitations;
- understanding beyond ordinary intelligence;
- existence beyond conventional forms.
