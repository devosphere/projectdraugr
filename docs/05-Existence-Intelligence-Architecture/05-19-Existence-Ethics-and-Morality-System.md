# Purpose

The Existence Ethics and Morality System defines how intelligent entities understand:

- right and wrong;
- harm and benefit;
- responsibility;
- justice;
- balance;
- consequences;
- coexistence.

This system establishes that morality is not a fixed universal law imposed upon all beings, but an evolving intelligence system developed through existence, consciousness, and experience.

---

# Core Principle

> **Morality is the understanding of how choices affect existence, consciousness, and balance.**

---

# Ethics vs Morality

Although related, ethics and morality represent different layers.

---

# Morality

Morality is internal understanding.

It answers:

"What do I believe is right?"

---

# Ethics

Ethics is collective understanding.

It answers:

"What principles allow beings to coexist?"
