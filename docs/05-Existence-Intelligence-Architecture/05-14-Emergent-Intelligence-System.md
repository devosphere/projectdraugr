# Purpose

The Emergent Intelligence System defines how intelligence can arise naturally from complex interactions without being directly designed or predetermined.

This system governs:

- unexpected intelligence development;
- consciousness emergence;
- species awakening;
- collective intelligence;
- civilization emergence;
- artificial emergence;
- unknown intelligence forms.

---

# Core Principle

> **Not all intelligence is created. Some intelligence emerges.**

Existence is not limited to predefined intelligent entities.

Through sufficient:

- complexity;
- interaction;
- experience;
- adaptation;
- memory;
- self-organization;

new forms of intelligence may naturally appear.

---


# Designed vs Emergent Intelligence

There are two major origins of intelligence.

---

# Designed Intelligence

Created intentionally by another intelligence.

Examples:

- artificial entities;
- engineered species;
- constructed beings.

---

# Emergent Intelligence

Developed naturally through existence.

Examples:

- evolving species;
- civilizations;
- collective consciousness.

---

# Emergent Intelligence Principle

The creator of a system does not always control everything that emerges from it.

Complexity creates possibility.

- does not evolve;
- does not learn;
- does not emerge;
- does not compete.

All emergent intelligence exists within what the Overseer observes.
