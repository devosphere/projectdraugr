# Purpose

The Entity Evolutionary Potential System defines how every existence within Project Draugr may change, develop, transform, or become something entirely new.

This system governs:

- individual growth;
- biological evolution;
- species development;
- intelligence progression;
- civilization advancement;
- transcendence;
- extinction;
- emergence of unknown existence forms.

The system exists to ensure that the universe is not populated by fixed entities with predetermined outcomes.

Instead:

> **Every existence carries potential futures shaped by interaction, experience, environment, and choice.**

# Final Principle
