# Purpose

The Existence Civilization Evolution System defines how groups of intelligent entities transform from primitive communities into advanced civilizations capable of shaping worlds, dimensions, and existence itself.

This system governs:

- civilization formation;
- cultural development;
- technological advancement;
- social evolution;
- political structures;
- civilization expansion;
- rise and collapse cycles;
- higher civilization states.

---

# Core Principle

> **A civilization is not defined by its technology, but by the maturity of its collective intelligence and its ability to coexist with existence.**
