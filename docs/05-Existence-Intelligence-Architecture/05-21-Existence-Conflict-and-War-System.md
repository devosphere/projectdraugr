# Purpose

The Existence Conflict and War System defines how disagreement, competition, opposition, and struggle emerge between intelligent entities.

This system governs:

- individual conflict;
- social conflict;
- species conflict;
- civilization wars;
- ideological conflicts;
- existential struggles;
- primordial conflicts;
- conflict resolution;
- the relationship between conflict and evolution.

---

# Core Principle

> **Conflict is not inherently destruction. Conflict is the interaction of opposing wills, beliefs, purposes, and existences.**

Conflict becomes destructive when balance, understanding, and responsibility are abandoned.

---

# Conflict Definition

Conflict occurs when:

Two Or More Existences
↓
Possess Incompatible Goals
↓
Attempt To Influence Reality
↓
Opposition Emerges
