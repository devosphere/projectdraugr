# Purpose

The Cultural and Civilization Intelligence System defines how individual intelligence evolves into collective intelligence.

This system governs:

- cultures;
- societies;
- civilizations;
- traditions;
- governments;
- economies;
- collective knowledge;
- societal identity;
- civilization evolution;
- civilization collapse and rebirth.

---

# Core Principle

> **A civilization is not merely a collection of entities. It is a living intelligence system created from the accumulated thoughts, experiences, creations, and choices of countless existences.**

Individual intelligence creates ideas.

Social intelligence connects individuals.

Cultural intelligence preserves meaning.

Civilization intelligence transforms meaning into history.

# Culture vs Civilization

Culture and civilization are related but different.

---

# Culture

The shared identity of a group.

Contains:

- beliefs;
- traditions;
- language;
- values;
- art;
- memories.

---

# Civilization

The organized structure that allows a culture to exist at scale.

Contains:

- institutions;
- technology;
- governance;
- economy;
- infrastructure.
