# Purpose

The Existence Transcendence and Evolution System defines the pathways through which entities, civilizations, and forms of consciousness transform beyond their current state of existence.

This system governs:

- evolution;
- transcendence;
- ascension;
- higher existence states;
- consciousness expansion;
- civilization advancement;
- artificial evolution;
- dimensional progression;
- existential transformation.

---

# Core Principle

> **Transcendence is not the abandonment of one's existence, but the evolution beyond previous limitations while preserving the essence that created it.**

---

# Evolution Definition

Evolution is the continuous process of change, adaptation, and development over time.

Evolution may occur through:

- biology;
- intelligence;
- technology;
- culture;
- consciousness;
- existence itself.

---

# Transcendence Definition

Transcendence is a transformation where an entity exceeds the limitations of its previous state.

Examples:

- biological limitations;
- cognitive limitations;
- dimensional limitations;
- existential limitations.
