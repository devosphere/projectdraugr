# Purpose

The Existence Immortality and Continuity System defines how entities maintain existence, identity, consciousness, essence, and influence across time.

This system governs:

- biological immortality;
- consciousness continuity;
- artificial continuity;
- identity preservation;
- legacy;
- eternal existence concepts;
- reincarnation concepts;
- persistence beyond physical form;
- limitations of immortality.

---

# Core Principle

> **Immortality is not merely the continuation of life, but the preservation of meaningful existence through change across time.**

---

# Immortality Definition

Immortality is the ability of an entity, pattern, consciousness, or influence to continue beyond ordinary limitations of lifespan.

Immortality may exist in different forms.

---

# Continuity Definition

Continuity is the preservation of connection between past, present, and future states of existence.
