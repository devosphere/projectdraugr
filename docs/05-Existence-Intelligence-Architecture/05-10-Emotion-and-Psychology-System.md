# Purpose

The Emotion and Psychology System defines the internal existence layer that influences how entities:

- experience reality;
- assign meaning;
- form attachments;
- develop personality;
- process suffering;
- create values;
- understand themselves and others.

This system governs the invisible internal world of entities.

While perception determines what an entity receives, and intelligence determines what an entity understands:

> **Emotion and psychology determine what an entity feels that understanding means.**

---

# Core Principle

> **Emotion is not a weakness of intelligence. Emotion is one of the mechanisms through which existence assigns significance.**

Without emotion:

- survival loses urgency;
- relationships lose meaning;
- choices lose personal consequence;
- experiences lose value.
