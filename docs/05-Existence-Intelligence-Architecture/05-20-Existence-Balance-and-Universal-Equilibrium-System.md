# Purpose

The Existence Balance and Universal Equilibrium System defines the fundamental principle that maintains the stability, continuity, and evolution of all existence.

This system governs:

- creation and destruction;
- growth and decay;
- order and chaos;
- expansion and contraction;
- life and death;
- stability and change;
- universal equilibrium.

---

# Core Principle

> **Existence survives through balance, not through absolute dominance of any single force.**

A universe without balance eventually collapses.
