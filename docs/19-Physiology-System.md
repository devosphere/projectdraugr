# 19 – Physiology System

> **Project Draugr**
>
> *The body obeys nature. Nature never negotiates.*

---

# Purpose

The Physiology System simulates the biological state of every Chronicle.

Unlike traditional RPGs, Project Draugr does not use abstract statistics such as Hit Points or Mana.

Instead, the simulation models the body's natural physiological processes.

The Physiology System governs:

- health
- injuries
- disease
- hunger
- thirst
- energy
- body temperature
- wetness
- bladder
- hygiene

The values generated by this system are displayed through the Body HUD.

---

# Design Philosophy

Project Draugr follows one immutable principle:

> **The body behaves like a real human body.**

The simulation never:

- delays consequences
- grants hidden recovery
- protects the player
- artificially balances survival

Every physiological process follows deterministic rules during the MVP.

---

# Relationship with the Body HUD

The Body HUD displays physiological information.

The Physiology System determines:

# Water Survival Rule

Water deprivation uses a fixed survival threshold.

## MVP Constant

**Maximum survival without water:** **73 hours**

After reaching:

> **73 hours + 1 second**

the Chronicle dies due to **Critical Dehydration**.

## Dehydration Progression

| Elapsed Time | State |
|--------------|-------|
| 0 hours | Hydrated |
| 24 hours | Thirsty |
| 48 hours | Dehydrated |
| 72 hours | Critical Dehydration |
| 73 hours + 1 second | Death |

---

# Food Survival Rule

Food deprivation uses a fixed survival threshold.

## MVP Constant

**Maximum survival without food:** **721 hours**

Equivalent to:

- 30 days + 1 hour

After reaching:

> **721 hours + 1 second**

the Chronicle dies due to **Critical Starvation**.

## Starvation Progression

| Elapsed Time | State |
|--------------|-------|
| 0 hours | Satisfied |
| 72 hours | Hungry |
| 168 hours | Very Hungry |
| 336 hours | Starving |
| 720 hours | Critical Starvation |
| 721 hours + 1 second | Death |

# Bladder

Represents urinary urgency.

States:

```text
Empty

Comfortable

Need to Urinate

Urgent

Critical
```

The bladder naturally fills over time according to hydration and fluid intake.

The player is expected to relieve themselves before reaching the critical threshold.

---

## Bladder Overflow Rule

When bladder capacity exceeds its maximum threshold:

The Chronicle automatically loses bladder control.

Immediately:

- Bladder resets to Empty.
- Hygiene decreases.
- Lower-body clothing becomes Wet.

No direct health damage occurs.

The resulting wetness and hygiene penalties remain until addressed.

# Knowledge and Diagnosis

The body always experiences physiological changes.

Understanding them depends upon the Chronicle's knowledge.

Example:

Without medical knowledge:

```text
Condition

Unknown Fever
```

With medical training:

```text
Condition

Respiratory Infection
```

With advanced medical knowledge:

```text
Condition

Severe respiratory infection.

High fever.

Elevated complication risk.
```

The condition itself never changes.

Only the Chronicle's interpretation improves.
